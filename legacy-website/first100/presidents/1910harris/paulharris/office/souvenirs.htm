SOUVENIRS

Paul Harris as we have already seen elsewhere, was an inveterate traveler and made many journeys to all parts of the Rotary empire. Wherever he went, he collected gifts and souvenirs to remind him of the visits he had made, the events he had attended and the people he had met.  We are not sure where some of the items in the Gallery came from or how they came into Paul Harris' possession, so if anyone can help with more information, please contact us at www.historycomment.org
