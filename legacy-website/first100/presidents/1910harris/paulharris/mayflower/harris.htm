Genealogy of Paul Harris

 

Although Paul Harris claimed that his ancestors came over with the 'Mayflower', the reference was probably not to a 'Harris' but to John Alden, the cooper with the Pilgrim Fathers. Tracing the Harris connection is as yet incomplete. An amateur genealogist in Florida, Lewis Harris, has researched the Harris family fairly comprehensively, and much of the following is based on his findings. Lewis is not related to Paul Harris but has been researching the name Harris.

Paul was born on April 19, 1868 in Racine, Wisconsin to George Harris and his wife, Cornelia, Bryan. Many Rotary publications give details of Paul Harris' immediate family, his parents and his grandparents, while another starting point is the 1880 US Census.

Using the IGI, we can trace the family back in outline as far as the early 18th century or possibly the late 17th century. Lewis Harris has used many local New England registers to fill in the gaps in the IGI coverage. This then is the probable line, so far:-

married a. Lydia Grose
b. SArah VINTON
Dec 5,1688 Concord
died Oct 24, 1716
Concord, Middlesex, Mass

2. WILLIAM HARRIS son of George and Sarah

Concord, Middlesex, Mass

died Mar 29, 1782 Concord, Mass
3. WILLIAM HARRIS son of William and Mary
born Mar 17, 1726/7 Concord, Mass
or Holden, Worcester, Mass

Jan 24,1749/50 Worcester, Mass
died Aug 15, 1797 W. Brattleboro, Vt
buried at The Flat At Farm,
West Battleboro, Vt

4. SALATHIEL HARRIS son of William and Patience
born Aug 12, 1759
Holden, Worcester, Mass

5. HOWARD HARRIS son of Salathiel and Susanna

Wallingford, Rutland, Vt

Wallingford, Vt

6. GEORGE HARRIS son of Howard and Susanna
born abt 1842 Wallingford, Vt

May 12, 1864

7. PAUL HARRIS son of George and Cornelia
born Apr 19, 1868 Racine, Wis
married JEAN THOMPSON
July 2, 1910 Chicago
died Jan 27,1947 Chicago

We acknowledge the work of Lewis Harris of Tampa Bay, Florida in compiling the above tree.

Basil Lewis
