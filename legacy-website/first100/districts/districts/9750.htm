THE DISTRICT 9750 HISTORY

Prepared by the district and not verified by Rotary Global History

 

There are almost two dozen districts in Australia, and three cover the greater Sydney area, capital of the state of New South Wales.

 

District 9750 incorporates the Sydney CBD, eastern suburbs, inner west and southern metropolitan area, southwest to Camden and Picton in the Macarthur region, and south through Wollongong to Kiama in the Illawarra region. It comprises over 50 clubs and near 2,000 Rotarians.
