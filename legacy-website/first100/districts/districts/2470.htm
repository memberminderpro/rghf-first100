THE HISTORY OF DISTRICT 2470, Greece

A Part of Our History of Rotary in Europe

Prepared by the district and not verified by Rotary Global History

 

Today, District 2470 consists of 72 Rotary Clubs with about 2.000 members.

 

The first Rotary Club to be founded in Greece is the Athens (central) Club, which has functioned since 1928 and is one among the first 100 Rotary Clubs in the world.

 

The District’s membership is growing by the year and so are the donations to the Rotary Foundation.

Each Club performs significant work in its local community as well as internationally, with considerable programs among which the digging and maintaining of fresh water wells in Kenya, which project was personally supervised by Greek Rotarians; and the traveling and staying for several weeks, of a Greek medical team consisting of Rotarian doctors and doctor's friends of Rotary in Ethiopia in order to offer medical assistance to the inhabitants, but also to convey to them the latest medical know how.

 

Similar programs will take place this year having as main goal to reduce children’s mortality.


In 2000 after the disastrous earthquake in Athens, the District undertook the initiative and the cost of building a kindergarten school, which was destroyed by the earthquake, in the municipality of Acharnai.

The school is being attended today by 80 children of pre-school age.


Today, at Nikos Gatsos village, Asea, Rotarians, inhabitants and friends are trying to heal the wounds which last year’s devastating fires left behind.

For this reason the District’s future major goal is to try to help the inhabitants by building and equipping a rural infirmary which was burned down by the fire.

Moreover, the restoration of a traditional two storey house is being planned, in Andritsena, which is going to be used as a community cultural center.


We hope that with the help of all the Rotarians, these goals will be accomplished during the current year, so that we can go on to other projects, loyal to the mission which we consciously have undertaken.

 

The main and permanent aim of our organization is a BETTER TOMORROW for all humanity, because our goal is to MAKE DREAMS REAL.

 

acknowledgements to the District Board, Clubs, Members and Webmaster of the District
