Presidents Elect, Nominees, and Assistant Governors gathered in Kalamazoo March 18-20 with RI President Elect Ray Klinginsmith and Governor Elect Eileen Jennings for training with DT Chuck Cusick. Past Governor Pam Harkema served as Lead Trainer again for the six district event. She leaves PETS after 8 years of service to the committee.
