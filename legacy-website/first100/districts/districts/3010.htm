THE DISTRICT 3010 HISTORY

Prepared by the district and not verified by Rotary Global History

 

R.I. District 3010 comprising of the National Capital Territory of Delhi, the revenue districts of Faridabad, Gurgaon, Rohtak, Rewari, Sonepat & Mohindergarh in Haryana, Ghaziabad and Gautam Budh Nagar in U.P. came into being on July 1, 1984 with 39 clubs as R.I. District 3010. It was renumbered 3010 in R.Y. 1991-92

 

The first club in this region, the Delhi, was started in 1938 and received its charter on January 18, 1939. By 1970, seven (7) more new clubs had been formed in this region. The 70s saw significant growth, and by July 1, 1984, there were thirty nine clubs in the District. At present there are 102 clubs with over 4000 Rotarians.

 

From the beginning, the District has been very active in fulfilling the objectives of Rotary, committed to the ideal of community service. Apart from the various activities undertaken by individual clubs, many projects initiated at the district level have made a significant impact in the community service avenue. Some of the major District projects are:

  1. The Institute Rotary Cancer Hospital
  2. R.I. District Rotary Education Foundation
  3. District 3010 Rotary Eco Foundation
  4. Rotary Blood Bank
  5. Rotary Literacy Foundation

Our District pioneered the Pulse Polio Programme in India and has also made substantial contributions to the Rotary Foundation. Youth represents the future of any community, and in keeping with this conviction, our District has laid emphasis in inculcating a spirit of service amongst the youth of District 3010. There are over 75 Interact and over 30 Rotaract Clubs in our District , each active in various community / youth projects.
