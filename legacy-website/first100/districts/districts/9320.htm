THE DISTRICT 9320 HISTORY

Prepared by the district and not verified by Rotary Global History

 

 

While not strictly a "history", members will be interested to see this map of the area covered by D9320.

 

Rotary District 9320 (Zone 10) encompasses the central area  of South Africa and Lesotho.

In particular:-

  • the southern part of the North West Province
  • the entire Free State Province
  • the entire Mountain Kingdom of Lesotho
  • the eastern part of the Northern Cape Province
  • most of the Eastern Cape Province

District 9320's 50+ clubs are spread over an area of some 250,000 sq. km [98,000 sq. m]. This is approximately the size of:-
  • the entire UK
  • the state of Oregon in the USA, or
  • the state of Victoria in Australia

Whilst District 9320 is by no means the largest of all the Rotary Districts around the world, it is still quite large enough to make its DG wish he/she were a direct descendant of Clark Kent.

District 9320 comprises clubs ranging in size from large ones in cities like Port Elizabeth to small ones in charming little country towns such as Colesberg.
