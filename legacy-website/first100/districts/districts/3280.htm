THE HISTORY OF DISTRICT 3280

A Part of Our History of Rotary in BANGLADESH

Prepared by the district and not verified by Rotary Global History

 

Previously Bangladesh was under district 329.

 

Finally, during the District 329 Conference held in Katmandu, Nepal in 1983, Rotary International decided to create District 328 which would take effect from 1 July 1984 and cover the entire geographical area of Bangladesh.

 

The office of the first district governor of this district was served by the late Rtn. Aziz-ul-huq of Rotary club of Comilla.

 

The historical inauguration of District 328 was held at TSC Auditorium, Dhaka university on July 16, 1984, where the former President of Bangladesh, the late Justice Abu Dyeed Chowdhury was present as Chief guest.

 

In 1991-92 District 328 was renumbered as R.I. District 3280

 

Submitted by Rtn. Mohammed Rezaul Karim Past President, Rotary Club of Chittagong Sagorika Past President, Rotaract Club of Chittagong Down Town RID 3280, Bangladesh.
