THE DISTRICT 4480 HISTORY

Prepared by the district and not verified by Rotary Global History
 

Clubs of the district
 
District 448 - plus a number zero in 1990 - was installed in julho/1977, comprising 31 clubs and 847 members, from District 451 (6 clubs: Cafelândia, Lins, Lins-North Pirajuí and Promise), and District 454 (25 clubs: Bariri, Barra Bonita, Barry, Hill, Cardoso, Catanduva, Western Star, Ferndale, Itápolis, Irapuã, Jales, Jahu, Jahu East, Mirasol, Mount Pleasant, New Granada, New Horizon, Olympia , Pindorama, Potirendaba, Santa Adelia Sta.Fé of South SJRio Black, Black SJRio North and Votuporanga).

District 4480 comes from successive districts, beginning in 1928, when it was created the D-63, which included clubs from Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay and Uruguay. In 1929, he created the D-72, which included only the Brazilian clubs. Then turn by redistricting, the district is above 4480: D-28 (1939), D-41 (1944), D-121 (1949), D-453 (1957), D-454 (1969) and D-448 (1977), which started in 1990 D-4480.

Today (June/2011) comprise the District 69 clubs, totaling 1,759 members.
Clubs Older: SJRio Black (1935), Catanduva (1936), Barry (1936, Jahu (1937), Lins (1943), Santa Adelia (1944), Mirasol (1946), Potirendaba 1946).

ROTARY CLUB OF SAN JOSE DO RIO PRETO / SP
FOUNDED IN 11/29/1935

The annals report that in 1935, Mr. James Roth made a visit to Sao Paulo, as a representative of Rotary International. He was senior officer of Empresas Brasileiras - Bond and Share - and in that capacity he visited Black River in October of that year. This was then the name of the city, where he met Jose Goncalves Gonzaga, local manager of Companhia Paulista de Forca e Luz, a powerful group affiliated with the electricity supplier to almost all countries in South America

Through information provided by Gonzaga Gonçalves, Mr. James Roth found that the conditions were excellent to settle in Rio Preto a Rotary club. And so, with your help, people began visiting representative of the city, aimed at organizing a group of people to the founding of the Club.
Organized list, Mr. James Roth met with the guests in the auditorium of City Hall on the evening of November 29, 1935, when he expounded the purpose of Rotary, deciding then to formalize the Rotary Club of Black River . At the same meeting was elected the first board.
The solemn inauguration took place on the night of December 4, 1935, at the Terminus Hotel, attended by officials, ladies and grated people, as well as the District Governor and representatives of the sponsoring club, Araraquara.

The minutes of the founding document was signed by the secretary-elect Alceu de Assis, a book that has charter signed by the representative of Rotary International, Mr. James H. Roth. The record reports that the founding meeting took place "in November 29, 1935," under the following preliminary ": a) territorial extension will be the district of Black River, b) the constitution and bylaws are the same as Rotary International, with few modifications, c) the weekly meetings will be Saturdays at 20 pm., the hotel Terminus lasting 1 ½ hours, d) the board meetings are given on the 1st Saturday of each month at the same venue, the 19 pm., e) a second board will exercise until June 30, 1936; f) the installation of the club will take place December 4, 1935; g) The elected board may provide for the membership of the club to the Rotary International; h) compared professions and activities of the 20 founding members; i) election by secret ballot of the first board.

As the minutes of the 2nd meeting was in December 4, 1935 the installation of the club, with inauguration of President J. Borges de Azevedo and other members of the 1st board, this club's delegation of Araraquara. It was the opportunity granted the title of "Honorary Member" Mr. James Roth.

According to the minutes of the 22th meeting, the delivery of the "Constitutional Charter" occurred on the night of May 1, 1936, the Governor of District 72, District then only Brazilian Armando de Arruda Pereira, this representation of the sponsor club.
