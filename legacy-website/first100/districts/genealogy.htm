THE GENEALOGY OF THE WORLD'S DISTRICTS


OF ROTARY INTERNATIONAL
 (An attempt to achieve the impossible)
 

TEST YOUR SKILLS AND KNOWLEDGE

The attached files are in Microsoft Excel and cover nearly all Districts within the relevant numerical range, according to the 2008/09 Rotary International Directory.

 

You will note that there are many blank spaces.

Study the legend at the foot of each Excel page and see if you can fill in the blanks.

Please contact us with any information that you have.

Please note that while all the information contained in the attached files (below) may not be correct, most details have been confirmed with independent sources including matching information contained in a number of adjoining districts.

 

Good luck in your attempts to help us.
