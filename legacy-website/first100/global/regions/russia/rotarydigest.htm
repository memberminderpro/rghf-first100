History of Rotary in Russia 
 
 

In 1997 the magazine of "Rotary Digest" was registered as mass media and published of "Telephone commercial service of St. Petersburg 008" JSC under a contract with Rotary Clubs "St. Petersburg" and "St. Petersburg - Neva" edition of 12 thousand copies in Russian and English./// Примечание: Для прочтения текстов выберите фото страницы, нажмите "Действия" и "Загрузить фотографию" на Ваш компьютер.
