Rotary Club of San Salvador,  the First Club of El Salvador

Rotary International District 4240

Part of our section on Central America

 
Founding members of the Rotary Club in Santa Ana, El Salvador. Photo taken in November 1928.
Top row: unknown, Secundino Turcios, Samuel Alvarez Angel, Jose Luis Mendez, Manuel Bolaños, Porfirio Giammattei, Carlos Valdes
Middle row: Alberto Valdes,Miguel Rivera, Antonio Reyes Jr., Bernardo Medina, Angel Maria Ayala, Rubistiano Caminos, Pierre Geoffroy, Pablo Rivera, Alberto Pacas
Sited: Pedro Valerio Vides, Adguino Tirillo, Carlos Menendez Castro, Francisco Antonio Reyes, Carlos Alvarez Angel, Hilario Menendez Castro, Isidoro Nieto, Gustavo Alvarez, William McEntee
On floor: Jorge Alvarez Angel, Antonio Fernandez.
