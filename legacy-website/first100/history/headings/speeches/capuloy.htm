Presentations by RGHF Speakers
I have attached here 7 pictures taken during the Membership Development and Avenue of Club Service Seminar for District 3820 held at the Mount Malarayat Golf and Country Club on September 15, 2007.
The presentation went smoothly and was well applauded by the audience from District 3820. They were so excited to hear that there are so many information from the RGHF that are not found in the RI website.  I promised to post 1 link everyday to a website that would help them fill their Rotary Information section of their weekly bulletin.
 
Newest RGHF Member and RC Metro Lipa President Arnold R. Capuloy introduces the Rotary Global History to the participants from RI District 3820 covering 11 provinces in the Philippines.
 
Pres. Arnold Capuloy talks about Our Rotary Foundation Newsletter and how he values each issue and keeps a compilation of its printed copy for the the Club.
