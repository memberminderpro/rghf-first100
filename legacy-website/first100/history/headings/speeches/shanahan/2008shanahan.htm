Presentations by RGHF Speakers
These are photos taken of Tom and Cindy Shanahan comfortably at work for RGHF at the RI District 9820 Conference, held at Frankston Victoria, Australia over the weekend of 28th February to March 02nd 2008.
 
The limited display returned a curiosity response from delegates who assured us that they would forward the material given to both their club president and their respective club historian.
 
Through the kind courtesy of the Conference chairman, many of the conference kits had the pamphlets on RGHF inserted.
