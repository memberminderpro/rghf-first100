    "Rotary/UN Day 2008"
Our RGHF board members are very involved.
PDG Helen Reisler, RGHF board member for zone 32 writes:

"I have 23 presenters coming in from all areas (California, Canada, Maine, England, the tri-state New York area, etc, I was so afraid that we would not get large enough audience (I had worked so hard on this for so many months that I didn't want it to be wasted) so I did a lot of PR & Publicity (as usual). To my surprise, we more than doubled the audience in the youth room alone. We now have 640 registered and, unfortunately had to turn away people at a point. I have attached the program for the High School audience.(The very interesting Main Program is in a separate room). I am so proud of our Rotary Youth Groups. You will notice the variety of meaningful projects they are involved in."
