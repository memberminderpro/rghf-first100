Presentations by RGHF Speakers

Past Chairman Matts Ingemanson to spoke about Paul Harris in Wallingford, VT where Paul Harris grew up. The location for the speech was in the school house where Paul went to school during his first 6 years.

 

The picture shows Matts together with Wallingford Rotarians from left to right: Jim Marquis, Russ Lattuca, Ann Lattuca, Matts, the bust of Paul Harris (in the background), Dave Ballou, Stan Wilbur and Homer Somers.

 

In the background, you see a display of many Paul Harris memorabilia that was donated to the Rotary Club of Wallingford by Paul's wife Jean after Paul had passed away.
