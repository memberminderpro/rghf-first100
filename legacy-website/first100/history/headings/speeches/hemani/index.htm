I was invited by RC Wadhwan from District 3060 for speaking on fellowships and more about RGHF.

 

They were not aware about eClubs. (Viman belongs to an eClub) They were amazed to know about it and were interested in knowing how to make up the meetings they lost in their local clubs.

 

They in the Rotary centennial year, came out with a perfume called CENTENNIAL. This was a special fragrance prepared by them. This year they came out with DREAM DUO. This time it’s a twin pack and have two bottles. Will send you details later. They routinely sell it in market for US$50 but for Rotarians it is US$ 15. And good thing is that they are going to donate significant amount of this income to TRF.
