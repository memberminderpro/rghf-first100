If your organization has an interest in a Rotary Global History presentation, please use our contact form. (travel and lodging are requested for all appearances)

The researchers, writers and board members of Rotary Global History are dedicated Rotarians and have a passion for the history of Rotary International, our most important philanthropic and peaceful organization.

 

When you spend as much time, as some of our officers, directors and historians do, on the preservation and creation of this project, you naturally want to talk about it. So, we do that! Our team members have given talks on a wide range of history topics at USA, UK and European clubs, and we are beginning to develop speakers who will be available for every club, district, or other Rotary organization.

 

Our presentations are excellent for district conferences, zone institutes or special club meetings or events. They can be tailored to foundation events to raise awareness of the fundamentals and values that created what we have today.
