Famous Honorary Rotarians

Many of the illustrious figures in world affairs have been and are Rotarians. 
Royalty and elected political officials have been drawn to Rotary's ideal of "Service Above Self."
 
Walt Disney, who brought some of the Harris tales to the movie screen, was an honorary member of the Rotary Club of Palm Springs, California, U.S.A.
 Another daring adventurer, Thor Heyerdahl, is an honorary member of the Rotary Club of Larvik, Norway.

Francis (Latin: Franciscus; born Jorge Mario Bergoglio on December 17, 1936) is the 266th and current Pope of the Catholic Church, elected on March 13, 2013. Mrs. Rajashree Birla, honorary Rotarian from two clubs Mumbai and Mulund, is a Director on the Board of all the major Aditya Birla Group ( $40 Billion group) of Companies; viz., Grasim, Hindalco, Aditya Birla Nuvo and UltraTech Cement Ltd. Additionally, Mrs. Birla serves as a Director on the Board of the Aditya Birla Group's International Companies spanning Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines and Egypt.      
Many more Rotarians have achieved prominence, imparting a measure of their good reputations to Rotary itself, but Rotary is not just an organization of the famous. It is an organization of men of goodwill, in which the 4-Way Test and the ideal of "Service Above Self" are the really important measures of fame.
