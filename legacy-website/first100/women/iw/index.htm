Webmaster's Notes

1. This project is a work in progress, commencing only in July 2011. Please come back regularly.

2. Our objective is to create historical records of every country in which Inner Wheel clubs exist.

Please click on the link to your country.

3. #1 = the order of first club in each country (in progress)

4. Information required:

a). Name of the first club of your country, its sponsoring club or person who was responsible for bringing Inner Wheel to your country, the charter date, the club number, the charter President, and names of twinning clubs, photographs (in jpg) and any other points of historical interest;
b). A description of each District in your country, when it was formed or created from other districts and a district map;
c). A brief description of the oldest 3 clubs in each district showing the above information for each club.

5. Guide: "Score a 10" - at the risk of offending webmasters but to act as a guide to what is a high quality history, the webmaster is allocating a score of 10 to those countries whose histories deserve recognition.

Please limit your information to a maximum of 500 words.
