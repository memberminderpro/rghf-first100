BELOW ARE SOME OF OUR RGHF MEMBERS
WHO HAVE BROKEN THE GLASS CEILING
March 2013, RI Director 12/14 Anne L. Matthews, an RGHF member, has been appointed by Rotary International President-elect Ron Burton to serve as the Vice President of Rotary International for the 2013-14 Rotary year. RIPE Ron, also an RGHF member, announced this appointment on Monday.
 
Anne is the first woman elected to serve as Vice President of Rotary International. Anne is also the first woman to be elected to serve both as a Trustee of The Rotary Foundation and The RI Board of Directors.
 
February 2011, RIBI Elects the first woman president of a major Rotary organization, RGHF member, RIBI Pres 13/14 Nan McCreadie.


March 2012, RGHF member RID Betsey Demaray is chosen as RI Treasurer for 2012/13
