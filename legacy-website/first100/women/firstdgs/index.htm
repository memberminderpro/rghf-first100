The German Rotary magazine "Der Rotarier," for April 1995. Pictured on the cover was the first incoming class of Women District Governors.

From left to right, are

 

Gilda R. Chirafisi (District 7230)

Mimi Altman (District 6440)

Donna Rapp (District 6310)

Olive P. Scott (District 7190)

Anne Robertson (District 6710)

Janet W. Holland (District 5790)

Reba Lovrien (District 5520), and Virginia B Nordby (District 6380)

 

They served from July 1, 1995 through June 30, 1996. PDG Mimi Altman, who was treasurer of 

 

Rotary Heritage and History International Fellowship, passed away in March of 2001.
