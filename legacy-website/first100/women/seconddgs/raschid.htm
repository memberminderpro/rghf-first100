Zone 33, District 7610, Tin Tin Nu Raschid, McLean, VA
 
Biographical Sketch of Past District Governor Tin Tin Nu Raschid


● District Governor 1996-97 (first woman District Governor from Virginia)

Appointments by the Rotary International Presidents.

● 2005-06 Appointed by RI President Carl Wilhelm Stenhammer to represent him at the District Conference in Alberta, Canada.
● 2005-06 Appointed by President Carl-Wilhem Stenhammer as
Chair of the RI Committee on Global Networking Groups (Fellowships and Rotary Action Groups)
● 2004-05 Appointed by President Glenn Estess to represent him at the District Conference of District 7850 in Lake Fairlee, VT
● 2004-05 Appointed by RI President Glenn Estess as the Vice Chair for RI Comm. on Fellowships.
● 2003-04 Appointed by RI Pres. Jonathan Majiyagbe as a member of the RI Comm. on Fellowships.
● 2002-03 Appointed by RI Pres. Bhichai Rattakul as the Zone Coordinator on World Comm.Service
● 2001-02 Appointed by RI Pres. Rick King as the Zone Coordinator for Environmental Concerns.
● 2000-01 Appointed by RI Pres. Frank Devlyn as the Zone Coordinator for Environmental Concerns.
● 1997-00 Chaired Decorations Committee and the Community Service Committee at the Zone Institute of Zones 33 & 34 in Savannah, GA, Dorado, PR, Raleigh,NC, Tampa FL

Presentations at RI Conventions, Zone Institutes and District Conferences:

● 2006 Appointed by RI Director Milton Jones to introduce the Rotary Foundation Chair PRIP Luis Giay at the Zone Banquet and Chair the Class Reunion
● 2006 RI Convention in Copenhagen/Malmo, Speech as the Chair at the Annual General Meeting of Fellowships
● 2006 District Conference of District 5370 in Alberta, Canada
● 2005 RI Convention in Chicago, Speech as the Vice Chair at the Annual General Meeting of Fellowships
● 2005 RI Convention in Chicago. Organized the Floral Display and demonstration of flower arrangements as Chair of the Rotarian Floral Designers Fellowship
● 2004 District Conference of District 7850 in Fairlee, VT.
● 2003 District Conference of District 6890 in Tampa, FL
● 2003 District Conference of District 6950 in Tampa, FL
● 2002 International Convention in San Antonio. Panel Discussion on Environmental Concerns
● 1997 District Conference of District 6920 & 6830 in Jacksonville, FL

Activities at in her District

• 2009-10 Chair, Extension Committee, Formed Ashburn,VA club. Received the District Governor’s Citation
● 2008-09 Chair, District “Water Management Committee” , Rotary Fellowship & Rotary Action Groups
● 2007-08 Chair, District “Water management Committee” , Rotary Fellowships & Action Groups
● 2006-07 Chair, District “Water Management Committee, Rotary Fellowships & R AGs
● 2005-06 Chair, District “Water Management Committee, Chair Poverty Alleviation
● 2005-06 Chair, the Centennial Banquet Committee (Forty of the 52 clubs in the District were
represented at the Banquet.)
● 2004-05 Chair, Poverty Alleviation Committee, “ Water Management”
● 2003-04 Chair, Poverty Alleviation Committee
● 2001-02 Chair, 50th Anniversary Banquet of Falls Church Club
● 1998-04 Organized the Joint meeting of luncheon clubs in the District
● 1998-99 District Trainer
● 1998-99 Chair, PDG Council
● 1998-99 Chair, Nominating Committee for DG,
● 1997-98 Chair, District Finance Committee; Chair, Public Relations Committee
● 1996-97 District Governor
● 1995-96 District Governor Nominee
● 1994-95 Governor’s Representative in charge of Area One.
● 1992-93 Chair, District Awards Committee; President of McLean Club
● 1991-92 Chair , District Awards Committee, President Elect/ Secretary of McLean club
● 1989-90 Chair, 25th Anniversary of McLean Rotary Club
● 1980-89 President of the Inner Wheel Club (wives of Rotarians) Washington DC
● 1969-80 President of the Inner Wheel Club of Karachi, Pakistan ( formed three other Inner Wheel Clubs and the First Rotaract Club in Karachi)
● 1958-69 Assisted father-in-law and husband when they were District Governor and President in Rangoon, Burma.
● Attended more than 45 District Conferences in 9 different Districts; 17 Zone Institutes (4 in Zones 31 &,32 and 13 in Zones 33&34), 5 International Institutes; 3 Presidential Conferences in Washington DC, Hong Kong and Toronto; and 13 International Conventions.

Awards Received:

As President - “Service Above Self” from District Governor Jack Kenyon;
“ District Foundation Award” from District Foundation Chair PDG Pete Petro
“ Presidential Citation” from RI President Dr. Clifford Dochtorman, as President of McLean Club in 1994
District 7610 was announced at the RI Convention in Glasgow, Scotland for achieving the Membership goal

As District Governor - Golden Century Award from RI President Luis Giay
Membership Achievement Award from RI Pres. Luis Giay
Special Annual Programs Fund Recognition from TRF Chair PRIP Raja Saboo.
Presidential Citation from RI President Luis Giay

As Past District Governor -2001-02 Presidential Citation as Distinguished Past District Governor, from RI President Rick King and receiving the Eagle award for Membership.

Received Rotary International’s most prestigious award of “SERVICE ABOVE SELF” in April 2006

● Tin Tin Nu & Bilal Raschid (received in 1997) are probably the only husband and wife in the Rotary world to have received this award as of 2006 ???.

● Both Tin Tin Nu Raschid and Bilal Raschid ( Rotarian of 48 years) are Major Donors, Sustaining Paul Harris Fellows, Benefactors and members of the Bequest Society.
