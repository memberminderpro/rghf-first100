MARY MARGARET FLEMING
Rotary, National, and Community Service Biography

A Rotarian since 1988, Charter Member and Past President of Monterey Peninsula Sunset Rotary Club (1991-92), Mary Margaret Fleming, holds membership in the Rotary Club of Monterey International. She served as District Governor in 1996-97, is a multiple Paul Harris Fellow, Benefactor, and maintains perfect attendance.

A Chicago native, resident in California for the past 29 years, Mary Margaret has a long history of community service. She also is a business consultant who designs/installs efficiency office systems; has coordinated national, state, and local political campaigns, fund raising campaigns, interior design (business and residential) and authored two books: Writing and Communicating and Steamed Rice. Additionally, Mary Margaret is a fine artist who holds a B.F.A. from the Art Institute of Chicago and invites you to visit her Fine Art web site: <www.marymargaretfleming.com>. Her daughter, Catherine, resides in Folsom, CA.
