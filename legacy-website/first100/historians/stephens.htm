Historians who created this project
Garth Stephens,* Fire & General Insurance Management, RC of Birkenhead, District 9910, New Zealand, Past President of Whangarei North Club 1975/1976, National Coordinator for New Zealand PolioPlus Campaign 1987/88, District 9910 Editor 1991/1998, District 9910 Secretary of J. R. McKenzie Youth Education Fund 1992/2000. Coordinator for New Zealand. Garth was the first member of our team to complete a section of this project by finishing the New Zealand histories in August of 2002. He was then voted a member of the 1905 Coin Society by the Executive Committee of Rotary's Global History  Project.

*18 May 1923 - 16 January 2003

"As with all organisations, it is important for Rotary to have ready access to its history from which may be gained a proper understanding of past achievements and the inspiration to pursue a purposeful course into an ever-changing future" Garth Stephens
