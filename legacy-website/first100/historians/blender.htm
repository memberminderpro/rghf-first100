PDG Edward (Eddie) Blender

Founding member, Director Emeritus & Senior Historian, RGHF Ambassador, Paul Harrisand the Rotary Foundation & Chair: Why I am RotarianDentistry,
RC of Edwards, Colorado USA, PDG 5470
Eddie is a founding member of this history website project. He has been a contributing editor, and founding member, since its inception in November of 2000.

www.1905society.org member of the Arch Klumph Society

2008 - TRF's DISTINGUISHED SERVICE AWARD and 2008 - RI's SERVICE ABOVE SELF AWARD

He is a board member emeritus of RGHF Eddie served as Vice President for the 2006-2007 year. "Rotarians learn from each other; we learn from the men and women who care enough to help build a better world. What better teachers are there then our predecessors in Rotary. Read their stories, read their accomplishments. You will be convinced, and a convinced leader becomes an extraordinary convincer. The farther back you can look, the farther forward you are likely to see."

PDG Blender was also recently appointed chair of the Rotary Centers Major Gifts Initiative.

﻿﻿﻿Eddie is a Past Governor (Colorado D5470) and has had local, national and international leadership, membership and Foundation experience that includes:
• Governor Elect Trainer (GETS) 2001, 2003
• Training Leader—International Assembly (Anaheim) 2002-03 and 2003-04
• Rotary Regional Foundation Coordinator (Z-25) 2002 to 2004
• TRF Representative - the National Association of Fellowship Advisors (NAFA) 2003-2005
• Literacy Task Force Coordinator (Z-25)
• Assistant Zone Membership Coordinator
• 3-H Grants Committee (India and Mexico)
• Country Representative (US and Canada) New Models for Rotary Clubs Pilot Project 2001 to 2007
· RI Presidential Representative (numerous conferences)
• Hope Alliance Country Selection Committee
• Rotary Volunteer
• Director, Rotary Global History
• President-elect, International Dental Volunteers
• Editor “Our Foundation Newsletter”
• Editor “What Paul Harris Said”
• Editor: “Why I am a Rotarian”
• District 5470 Rotary Scholarship Committee
• RI Council on Legislation 2004 Representative
• Founding Member: United Way—Charter Committee (Eagle County)

Eddie and his wife Patti are Rotary Foundation Charter Bequest Society members, Paul Harris Society Members, Major Donors and have Endowed a Rotary World Peace Scholarship.
They have recently been made members of the Arch Klumph Society.
