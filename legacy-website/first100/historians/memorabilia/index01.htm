A COLLECTION OF OTHER MEMORABILIA
The majority of these items appear to have come from the private collection of 1987/88 RI President Charles C. Keller
and were auctioned in February 2009, as noted below
Others have been found at internet sites around the world
We hope you find them interesting

See also the beautiful Silver Jubilee Water Jug which is shown in the photo of Paul Harris' Desk.


 PAGE 1 OF 3

Bronze Medallion with Rotary International Symbol "Rotarians United in Service Dedicated to Peace 1987/88" on back. Quartz from Brazil R.I. symbol mounted on it. Gift from Antonio Augusto & Brunilde, Governor 87-89 of District 453 in Brazil. Rotary International Commemorative Medallion Signature of Paulo V. C. Costa, President '90-'91 on back. One Troy Oz .999 Fine Silver Coin from San Antonio Honoring the 75th anniversary of Rotary Club No. 52 in San Antonio, TX.
1982 Rotary Medallion from Greece from District 247. Polio Plus Commemorative Pewter Medallion Honoring "Rotary's Gift to the Children of the World" monument, dedicated June 28, 1991. No. 367.

Polio Plus Commemorative Silver Medallion Minted for the Mid-Jersey Cape Rotary Club of Southern New Jersey, District 764. Dated 1987-1988. Minted with the purpose of raising money for Polio Plus.
 

 Arch C. Klumph Commemorative Bronze Coin From the "Home District" of Klumph, District 6630 in Northeast Ohio.
Stained Glass Rotary Symbol Plaque reads: "Chuck Keller; D 510; 8 June 1988", 1988 Rotary Int'l Bucks Convention in Philadelphia Commemorative bills from Ollie Hammond, P.D.G. Gov. 1969-70. District 528, California Rock Carving from Norway Gift from District 231 in Olso, January 1987. An explanation of the carving is given on the back. Hand Painted Glass Decorative Plate From Orscelik Balkn, Gov. D 243, Turkey.
Hand Carved Wood Hanging from Chile Signed "Arevalo". Gift from District 498 from Maldonado and D 434 from Florida during a 4-district conference in Santiago Chile. Commemorative Plate Asia Regional Conference in Seoul, Korea; 20-23 September, 1979. 75th Anniversary Commemorative Plate Made in Rorstrand, Sweden . Designed by Rotarian Gunnar Nylund. Commemorative Silver Plate Tray "In appreciation for your outstanding leadership demonstrated at the R.I. Asia Zone Four Institute held December 12-14, 1986 at Tapei, Taiwan, Rep. of China" From Gary C. K. Huang, Governor of D 345 in Hong Kong East Island.
