Join the Paul Harris Room 711 Club

 

Help preserve the birthplace of Rotary – become a lifetime member of the Paul Harris 711 Club

In addition to its efforts to preserve Room 711, the Paul Harris 711 Club helps to support Rotary’s History and Archives, which provides access to Rotary artifacts, photographs and documents. These materials are used in exhibits, stories and research that celebrate and share Rotary’s rich history.

You can help sustain Room 711 by contributing US $100 to become a lifetime member of the Paul Harris 711 Club. As a member, you will receive a certificate, lapel pin, and Room 711 souvenir key. Click on this link to provide your membership information and to pay by credit card. Membership materials will be sent to you via postal mail.

Gift memberships may be ordered to be delivered to another person. Please contact Betty Clementz, 711 Club Secretary, directly at tbclementz@gmail.com to obtain the form to submit information and payment directly to her. Do not enter the information on this website.
