The Charter Banquet of the Tri-City Rotary Club on February 26, 1909, at the Athens Hotel in Oakland. The speakers of the evening are seated against the wall in the back. They include H.C. Capwell, (Department store owner) and Tri-City charter member, Ng Poon Chew, (author, publisher, and advocate for Chinese American civil rights. He published the first Chinese language daily newspaper to be printed outside of China. He was known as the Chinese Mark Twain), Oakland Mayor Frank Mott, Frank Bilger (Paving and Cement), the Club's first President. Homer Wood, the founder, and San Francisco Rotary's President also spoke.
