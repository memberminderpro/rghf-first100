Histories of Clubs Hosting RI Conventions

Rotary Club of Las Vegas, Nevada

Rotary International District 5300

Host of the RI Convention of 1986
Las Vegas Rotary has a history of more than 80-years of service in the Southern Nevada area.  Rotary’s high ideals of friendship, fellowship, international understanding, and service are applied in personal, business, and community life by the over 166 members of our Las Vegas Rotary Club.

LAS VEGAS Rotary Global History
 
Las Vegas Rotary Founded April 5, 1923.
 
First Meeting at Chocolate Shop at 203 Fremont Club, next met at the Beanery in Union Pacific Station. Moved to Kiva Club in basement of the Apache Station Hotel.

El Rancho Vegas provided first strip location in 1941.

    • 1944 moved to Last Frontier
    • 1957 moved to Tropicana Hotel
    • 1974 moved to Landmark
    • 198? moved to Holiday (Harrah’s)
    • 1995 moved to Desert Inn
    • 2000 moved to Lawry’s

Original Las Vegas Club had 17 members. Current membership is 166 active members.
