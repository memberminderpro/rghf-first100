THE HISTORY OF INTERACT
IN PUERTO RICO
  Mr. Mario F. Gazfambide from Rio Piedras was one of the 5 members of the 1961-62 ad hoc Committee on Youth and authors of the Interact Plan.

 The Rotarian on June 1977 reported that the Interact Club of Corozal sponsored a summer camp for boys and girls aged from 8 to 12.
